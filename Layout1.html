Flexbox trick:

Make containers a min-height: 100vh so that children inherit this height and stretch to fill vertical space.

Note: vh units are not supported on older browsers.

This is for desktop sizing, (> 550px).

Mobile-first version not currently stretching to full screen height. This may not be that much of an issue!
