Buffalo Point Trip - July 9-11, 2025

The forecast on the previous week had it raining for the Wednesday through Friday, but the weather turned out beautifully. Wednesday was warm and sunny, on Thursday we played 36 and it was really hot and humid in the afternoon. Friday was very smokey due to forest fires on the way from Buffalo Point to Steinbach but visibility got better as our round went on.
