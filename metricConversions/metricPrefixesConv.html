Metric Converstions

The metric prefixes are symbols that represent powers of 10 as follows:

f, femto (10-15)
p, pico (10-12)
n, nano (10-9)
u, micro (10-6)
m, milli (10-3)
c, centi (10-2)
d, deci (10-1)
(base), (100)
da, deca (101)
h, hecto (102)
k, kilo (103)
M, mega (106)
G, giga (109)
T, tera (1012)
P, peta (1015)

Instructions

Enter any valid number, then choose the "from" and "to" prefixes you want. Adding a base unit is optional. Click Convert to see the result below.

If you don't want a prefix for one of your conversions (your number involves a base unit), simply do not selected a radio button. Use the Clear button to remove previous selections.
